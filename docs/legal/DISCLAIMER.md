# Disclaimer

This project provides live AI-generated captions as an accessibility aid. It is not a guarantee of complete, accurate, or legally compliant accessibility provision.

## Caption accuracy

AI-generated captions can be wrong, delayed, incomplete, or misleading. Accuracy may be affected by microphone quality, background noise, reverberation, accents, overlapping speech, music, unusual names, Bible references, theological words, and sound-desk setup.

For high-stakes settings such as funerals, safeguarding training, formal legal or disciplinary meetings, confidential pastoral conversations, counselling, or situations where accuracy is essential, churches should consider qualified human captioners, sign-language interpreters, or other appropriate professional support.

## Not a replacement for pastoral judgement

Operators should pause, blank, or stop captions during sensitive pastoral moments, private prayer, safeguarding disclosures, testimony involving vulnerable people, or other confidential situations.
